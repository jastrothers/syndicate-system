# Loot Deck (The Reward)

Loot cards are gained primarily during Phase III: Execution by overcoming Obstacles. During Phase IV: Extraction, players tally their VP from extracted Loot.

1. **Cash Stacks**
   - **VP Value:** 1
   - **Special Rule:** Easy to carry. When calculating Extraction success, Cash does not count towards the "drop" penalty (you choose to drop it or not, but it doesn't fulfill the requirement to drop your lowest/highest reward).

2. **Gold Bullion**
   - **VP Value:** 3
   - **Special Rule:** Heavy. Adds +1 to your personal Extraction Difficulty (calculated after the group Heat base calculation).

3. **Encrypted Data Drive**
   - **VP Value:** *Variable*
   - **Special Rule:** 1 VP per Data Drive extracted by the Crew (Set Collection). E.g., if the crew extracts 3 Data Drives total, each is worth 3 VP.

4. **Stolen Art Masterpiece**
   - **VP Value:** 5
   - **Special Rule:** Fragile. If you must drop a reward during a Partial Success or Failure in Phase IV, you MUST drop the Stolen Art first.

5. **Bearer Bonds**
   - **VP Value:** 2
   - **Special Rule:** Reliable. No special modifiers or extraction penalties.

6. **Jewelry Cache**
   - **VP Value:** 2
   - **Special Rule:** Fencing. At the end of the game, if you have the most Jewelry Caches, gain +2 VP.
