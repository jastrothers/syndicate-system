# Archetype: The Driver

*Focuses on the extraction phase, reducing risks when the alarm goes off.*

## Base Stats

- **Starting Health:** 4
- **Base Hand Size:** 5
- **Passive Skill:** *Idling Engine* - You may ignore 1 "Roadblock" penalty during The Getaway.

## Starting Deck (10 Cards)

### 4x Wrench

- **Type:** Starting Card
- **Effect:** Provides 1 Gear

### 3x City Maps

- **Type:** Starting Card
- **Effect:** Provides 1 Intel

### 2x Evasive Maneuvers

- **Type:** Starting Card
- **Effect:** Reduce the Heat cost of your next action (or Obstacle) by 1.

### 1x Nitro

- **Type:** Starting Card (Loud)
- **Effect:** Draw 2 cards, then discard 1.
