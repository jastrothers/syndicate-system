# Archetype: The Grifter

*Focuses on social engineering, disguise, and distraction to bypass human elements.*

## Base Stats

- **Starting Health:** 4
- **Base Hand Size:** 5
- **Passive Skill:** *Inside Man* - You may peek at the top card of the Obstacle Deck once per turn.

## Starting Deck (10 Cards)

### 4x Smooth Talk

- **Type:** Starting Card
- **Effect:** Provides 1 Intel

### 3x Concealed Carry

- **Type:** Starting Card
- **Effect:** Provides 1 Gear

### 2x Misdirection

- **Type:** Starting Card
- **Effect:** Ignore 1 Intel or Gear requirement of a "Human" Obstacle.

### 1x Bribe

- **Type:** Starting Card (Loud)
- **Effect:** Provides 1 Intel, 1 Gear, generates 1 Heat.
