## Mechanical Overview

The core mechanical framework for Atomic Frontiers is built on a Story Driven branching narrative, powered by a tactical Crew Assignment (Worker Placement) system. Players resolve challenges via Atomic Checks (Dice Rolling) using 2d6, supplemented by versatile Multi-Use Gadget Cards. The game follows a Fixed Turn Order to emphasize narrative momentum.

## Thematic Brief

# Thematic Brief: Atomic Frontiers

**Set in a vibrant, 1950s-inspired "Raygun Gothic" timeline**, *Atomic Frontiers* puts players at the helm of the *Astra-V* rocket ship. Armed with bubble-helmets and humming rayguns, your crew explores the mysterious **Glimmer Sector**—a celestial frontier filled with neon nebulae, crystalline planets, and the ever-present threat of the Void Syndicate.

The game captures the spirit of classic space-opera serials. Every choice you make in the **Expedition Log** ripples through the sector, while your ability to assign the right crew to the right **Station** determines if you'll survive a meteor storm or successfully negotiate with the Nebula Clans. It’s an age of chrome, atoms, and infinite adventure!

## setup

### Game Setup

# Setup: The Astra-V Mission

1. **Sector Prep**: Place 3 **Sector Board Tiles** in the center of the table. Connect them to form the initial 'Glimmer Sector' map.
2. **Dashboard Config**: Each player takes a **Ship Dashboard** and places the chrome **Star-Dust Tracker** on the start of the 'Progress' track.
3. **Crew Mobilization**: Each player chooses a color and takes the 4 matching **Crew Tokens** (Captain, Science Officer, Pilot, Cadet).
4. **Logbook Initialization**: Open the **Expedition Log** to Entry #1: *The Glimmer Threshold*.
5. **Arsenal Ready**: Shuffle the **Gadget Deck** and deal 4 cards to each player.
6. **Token Pool**: Place **Atom-Tape** tokens in a common pile (The Stockpile).
7. **Order of Operation**: The player who most recently saw a classic sci-fi movie goes first.


## rules

### How to Play

# The Mission Routine (Turn Structure)

A game of *Atomic Frontiers* is played over a series of mission phases. On your turn, follow the **Operational Sequence**:

### 1. Station Assignment (Crew Placement)
Assign any unassigned **Crew Tokens** to unoccupied **Duty Stations** on your Ship Dashboard or at Exploration Hubs on the current Sector Tiles.
- **Ship Stations**: Provide passive bonuses or resource generation.
- **Exploration Hubs**: Required to trigger the Narrative phase.

### 2. The Log Phase (Narrative Action)
If you have a crew member at an Exploration Hub, read the corresponding entry in the **Expedition Log**. You must make a choice based on your current crew assignments and hand of Gadgets.

### 3. Atomic Flux Phase (Resolution)
If your choice requires a check, roll 2d6 and add any bonuses from your assigned Crew and equipped Gadgets. Compare to the check difficulty in the Log entry.
- **Success (8+)**: Gain rewards and advance the Star-Dust Tracker.
- **Setback (5-)**: Narrative loss or take "Sector Stress" tokens.

### 4. Gadget Calibration (End of Turn)
Draw 1 card from the **Gadget Deck**. If you have more than 7 cards, you must discard or equip a card.
