## Core Mechanics

Five interlocking mechanisms drive the nursery experience:\n\n**Hand Management** — Draw 5 cards each turn. Choose which care cards to play on nursery Pokémon, which to hold, and which to discard. No carry-over between turns.\n\n**Deck Building** — Start with 10 basic cards. Purchase specialized care cards (Berry, Medicine, Song, Toy) and baby Pokémon from a shared market row of 5 cards (+1 Pokémon slot). Standard shuffle-when-empty cycling.\n\n**Set Collection** — Each baby Pokémon requires a specific combination of 3-5 care icons to evolve. Care cards played are tucked under the Pokémon, preserving progress across turns. Playing 3+ of the same type triggers a Nurture Burst bonus.\n\n**Tableau Building** — The nursery board starts with 3 slots (expandable to 5). Each slot holds one baby Pokémon accumulating care. Evolved Pokémon remain as scoring anchors with ongoing abilities. Up to 3 facility upgrades available.\n\n**Income** — Base 2 care tokens/round, +1 per baby Pokémon, +1 per evolved Pokémon (stacking), facility bonuses. Token cap of 8.\n\n**Core Loop**: Nurture Phase (income) → Supply Phase (draw + market) → Care Phase (play cards) → Growth Phase (evolve + adopt) → Cleanup Phase.

## Overview

**PokéNursery: Blissful Beginnings**
Welcome to Blossom Grove Nursery, where Chansey caretakers tend to baby Pokémon, nurturing them with berries, medicine, songs, and toys until they are ready to evolve. Build your care repertoire, expand your nursery, and earn the most Reputation to become the Grand Blossom Caretaker.

- **Players**: 1-4
- **Playtime**: 35-70 minutes (scales with player count)
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Core Mechanisms**: Daily Schedule (Hand Management), Blossom Bazaar (Deck Building), Care Combos (Set Collection), Nursery Wing (Tableau Building), Morning Provisions (Income)

## Setup

**For All Player Counts:**

1. Place the **Blossom Bazaar board** in the center of the table.
2. Shuffle all 56 **Bazaar Care Cards** (14 Berry + 14 Medicine + 14 Song + 14 Toy) into a single face-down deck. Place on the Care Card Draw Pile space.
3. Shuffle all 16 **Baby Pokémon Cards** into a face-down deck (baby side up). Place on the Baby Pokémon Draw Pile space.
4. Reveal Baby Pokémon from the draw pile to fill the **Meadow** slots: 2 slots at 1-3 players, 3 slots at 4 players.
5. Deal Care Cards face-up to fill the **Market Row**: 5 slots at 1-3 players, 6 slots at 4 players.
6. Shuffle all 12 **Facility Cards** into a face-down deck beside the board. Reveal the top 2 face-up — these are available for purchase.
7. Place all 40 **Care Tokens** (30 x 1-value, 10 x 5-value) in a shared supply.
8. Each player takes:
   a. 1 **Nursery Wing** board. It has 5 Crib slots: 3 unlocked (available immediately), 2 locked (unlocked by Crib Expansion facilities).
   b. 1 **Starting Deck** of 10 cards: 3 Gentle Touch, 2 Berry Snack, 2 Soothing Lullaby, 2 Rattle Toy, 1 Potion Drops. Shuffle and place face-down on the Deck Zone.
   c. 1 **Reputation Tracker** meeple on the 0 space of the Reputation Track.
   d. 1 **Quick Reference Card**.
9. Each player takes **2 Care Tokens** (1-value each) from the shared supply.
10. Draw **5 cards** from your personal draw pile. This is your first **Daily Schedule**.

**Solo Setup (additional steps):**
11. Shuffle the 12 **Season Clock Cards** and place in a face-down stack. The Round Tracker tracks the current Day (1-12).
12. Do not use the Start Caretaker Egg.

**Multiplayer First Player:**
Most recently cared for a pet or child receives the Start Caretaker Egg. Fallback: youngest player. The Round Tracker is not used in multiplayer.

## Turn Structure

A game is played over **Weeks** (rounds). Each Week, every player takes one **Day** (turn) clockwise from the Start Caretaker Egg holder. Each Day has 5 phases in strict order:

**Phase 1: Morning Provisions (Income)**
Income = 2 (base) + 1 per Baby Pokémon in Cribs + 1 per Evolved Pokémon in Cribs + Facility bonuses.
**Trailing Caretaker Bonus**: If you have 2+ fewer evolutions than the leader, gain +1 Care Token per evolution deficit (max +3 bonus).
Take tokens from supply. Reserve cap: 12 tokens. Excess income is lost.

**Phase 2: Supply Run (Draw)**
If personal draw pile is empty, shuffle discard pile into a new draw pile. Draw up to 5 cards (hand limit). Pikachu ability: +1 card. Melody Room facility: +1 card. If draw pile and discard are both empty, play with however many cards you have.

**Phase 3: Caring Hands (Main Actions)**
Perform any number of actions in any order:
- **Play a Care Card**: Tuck a card from hand under a Baby Pokémon. Icons count toward evolution. Gentle Touch = 1 wild icon (declare type when tucked).
- **Purchase a Care Card**: Pay printed cost (1-3 tokens). Take from Market Row to personal discard. Refill Market Row from draw pile.
- **Adopt a Baby Pokémon**: Take from Meadow to an empty unlocked Crib (free). Refill Meadow from Baby Pokémon Draw Pile.
- **Purchase a Facility**: Pay printed cost (2-4 tokens). Place in empty Facility slot (max 3). Refill display.
- **Spring Cleaning** (new): Pay 2 Care Tokens to remove 1 card from your hand permanently (out of the game). This thins your deck.
- **Pass**: Stop taking actions voluntarily.

**Phase 4: Growth Check (Evolution)**
Check each Baby Pokémon: if tucked icons meet or exceed ALL printed requirements, it evolves. Flip card to evolved side (Evolution Glow). Discard all tucked cards to personal discard. Gain Reputation equal to printed value. Evolved ability activates next Day. Check end-game trigger after all evolutions resolve.

**Phase 5: Dusk Cleanup**
Discard all remaining hand cards. Check Blossom Greenhouse (3+ cards played = gain 1 token). Refill Meadow to full if any slots are empty. Pass turn clockwise.

**End of Week**: After all players complete a Day, pass Start Caretaker Egg clockwise. New Week begins.

## Action Catalog

All actions occur during the Caring Hands phase unless otherwise noted.

| Action | Cost | Effect | Restrictions | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Play a Care Card | 0 tokens (card from hand) | Tuck under Baby Pokémon; icons count toward evolution | Must target Baby Pokémon (not Evolved). Card must be in Daily Schedule. | Caring Hands |
| Purchase a Care Card | 1-3 tokens | Take from Market Row to personal discard; refill row | Sufficient tokens required. Cannot buy from draw pile. | Caring Hands |
| Adopt a Baby Pokémon | 0 tokens | Take from Meadow to empty Crib; refill Meadow | Must have empty unlocked Crib. Meadow must have a baby available. | Caring Hands |
| Purchase a Facility | 2-4 tokens | Place in empty Facility slot; active immediately | Must have empty Facility slot (max 3). | Caring Hands |
| Spring Cleaning | 2 tokens | Remove 1 card from your hand permanently (out of game) | Must have a card in hand. Cannot remove the last card in hand. | Caring Hands |
| Declare Gentle Touch Type | 0 (part of Play) | Choose care type for wild icon | Declare at time of tucking; permanent. | During Play action |
| Pass | 0 | End Caring Hands voluntarily | Unspent tokens remain in reserve. | Caring Hands |

**Hybrid Card Special Effects:**
- Herbal Compress: Draw 1 card when tucked.
- Sing-Along Story: May tuck under any Pokémon regardless of type match.
- Musical Mobile: +1 Reputation if it completes an evolution.
- Pecha Berry Remedy: 1 Berry + 1 Medicine icons (no extra effect).

## Scoring & Endgame

**Game End Trigger:**
- 2-3P: 8 total Evolved Pokémon across all players. Complete current Week, then play 1 final complete Week.
- 4P: 6 total Evolved Pokémon (same procedure).
- Solo: After 12 Days (Season Clock).

**Scoring Sources:**
1. Evolution Reputation (during play): Printed value per Evolved Pokémon (2-5 Rep).
2. Facility Reputation (game end): Printed value per Facility (1-2 Rep).
3. Togetic Bonus (during play): +1 Rep per evolution after Togetic evolved (maximum 3 bonus Rep total).
4. Musical Mobile Bonus (during play): +1 Rep per evolution completed by Musical Mobile.
5. Leftover Tokens (game end): 3 unspent tokens = 1 Rep (rounded down).
6. Care Card Collection Bonus (game end): 1 Rep per complete 4-type set tucked under un-evolved Pokémon.
7. No penalty for un-evolved Baby Pokémon.

**Tiebreakers (in order):**
1. More Evolved Pokémon.
2. Fewer total cards in personal deck (draw + discard + hand).
3. Most recent evolution (latest in turn order).
4. Shared victory.

**Solo Scoring Thresholds:**
| Reputation | Rating |
|---|---|
| 0-9 | Novice Caretaker |
| 10-14 | Bronze Caretaker |
| 15-19 | Silver Caretaker |
| 20-24 | Gold Caretaker |
| 25+ | Grand Blossom Caretaker |

## Special Rules & Keywords

**Nurture Burst**: Tuck 3+ cards of the same care type under 1 Baby Pokémon in 1 Day = +1 bonus icon of that type. Checked per Pokémon, per type, per Day. Gentle Touch counts for declared type. Lucario reduces threshold to 2 (all types). Toy Workshop reduces threshold to 2 (Toy only). Reductions do not stack below 2.

**Evolution Glow**: When a Baby Pokémon's care requirements are fully met during Growth Check, flip to evolved side. Remove all tucked cards to personal discard. Gain printed Reputation. Evolved ability active next Day.

**Trailing Caretaker Bonus**: During Morning Provisions, if you have 2+ fewer evolutions than the player with the most evolutions, gain +1 Care Token per evolution deficit (max +3 bonus tokens). This is in addition to normal income.

**Spring Cleaning**: Pay 2 Care Tokens during Caring Hands to permanently remove 1 card from your hand (out of game, not to discard). Thins your deck for future draws. Cannot remove your last card in hand.

**Evolved Pokémon Abilities (16):**
- Togetic: +1 Rep per subsequent evolution (maximum 3 bonus Rep total from this ability).
- Pikachu: Draw +1 card during Supply Run.
- Clefairy: 1/Day, treat 1 Song icon as any type.
- Jigglypuff: When tucking Song card, also tuck top card of draw pile face-down (counts as 1 wild icon).
- Hitmonchan: 1/Day, pay 1 token to tuck a card from discard pile instead of hand.
- Jynx: 1/Day, look at top 3 of Care Card Draw Pile, put 1 on top.
- Electabuzz: 1/Day, 1 Medicine card provides +1 Medicine icon.
- Magmar: Your Pokémon requiring 4+ icons need 1 fewer (minimum 3).
- Chansey: +1 Care Token during Morning Provisions.
- Roselia: 1/Day, gain 1 token when tucking a Berry card.
- Lucario: Nurture Burst threshold = 2 for all Pokémon.
- Snorlax: 1/Day, 1 Berry icon counts as 2.
- Marill: 1/Day, swap 1 hand card with top of draw pile.
- Sudowoodo: Facilities provide +1 Rep each at game end.
- Mr. Mime: 1/Day, copy another Evolved Pokémon's ability in your Cribs. If Mr. Mime is your only evolved Pokémon, you may instead peek at the top 2 Baby Pokémon cards and rearrange them.
- Chimecho: 1/Day, gain +1 Rep when triggering Nurture Burst.

**Facility Effects (6 types):**
- Crib Expansion (cost 3): Unlock 1 additional Crib. 1 Rep.
- Berry Garden (cost 2): +1 income during Morning Provisions. 1 Rep.
- Melody Room (cost 2): Draw +1 card during Supply Run. 1 Rep.
- Toy Workshop (cost 3): Nurture Burst threshold = 2 for Toy. 2 Rep.
- Healing Ward (cost 3): 1/Day, when you tuck a Medicine card, also add +1 Medicine icon. 1 Rep.
- Blossom Greenhouse (cost 4): If 3+ cards played in Caring Hands, gain 1 token at Dusk Cleanup. 2 Rep.

**Wild Icons**: Gentle Touch = 1 icon of any type. Declare when tucked; permanent.

**Care Token Cap**: Maximum reserve of 12 tokens at all times.

**Solo Season Clock**: Days 1-4 (First Light): no removal. Days 5-8 (Growing Pains): remove 1 Market Row card after refill. Days 9-12 (Grand Blossom): remove 2 cards after refill.

## Edge Cases & Clarifications

| # | Situation | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Care Card Draw Pile empty | Market Row not refilled. Empty slots remain. Players may still buy remaining face-up cards. |
| 2 | Baby Pokémon Draw Pile empty | Meadow not refilled. Adopt action unavailable when Meadow empty. |
| 3 | Personal draw + discard both empty | Draw 0 cards. Play Day with 0 hand cards. May still use token-based actions (Purchase, Adopt). |
| 4 | All Cribs occupied | Cannot adopt. Evolved Pokémon still occupy Cribs. Must buy Crib Expansion for new slots. |
| 5 | No legal actions in Caring Hands | Pass. Day continues to Growth Check and Dusk Cleanup. |
| 6 | Simultaneous ability triggers | Player chooses resolution order. Resolve each fully before next. |
| 7 | Sing-Along Story usage | Provides Song + Toy icons. May tuck under any Pokémon. Non-matching icons still count. |
| 8 | Mr. Mime copies ability | Copies effect only, not identity. No retroactive bonuses. Choose target each Day. If only evolved Pokémon, use fallback peek ability instead. |
| 9 | Multiple purchases per Day | Yes, unlimited Purchase actions per Day. Each resolved individually. |
| 10 | Facility deck empty | No refill. Buy remaining face-up cards only. |
| 11 | Reputation exceeds 30 | Game continues. Track above 30 with reminder token. |
| 12 | Magmar + 3-icon Pokémon | Warmth only reduces 4+ icon requirements. 3-icon Pokémon unaffected. |
| 13 | Spring Cleaning on last card | Cannot Spring Clean if it would leave you with 0 cards in hand. |
| 14 | Trailing Caretaker Bonus tie | Only triggers if you are 2+ behind the leader. If tied for most evolutions, no bonus. |
| 15 | Meadow refill timing | Meadow refills immediately when a baby is adopted AND at Dusk Cleanup if any slots empty. |

## Quick Reference Card

**Day Summary:**
1. Morning Provisions — Collect tokens (2 + babies + evolved + facilities, cap 12). Trailing bonus: +1/deficit if 2+ behind leader (max +3).
2. Supply Run — Draw to 5 cards
3. Caring Hands — Play cards, buy cards/facilities, adopt babies, spring clean (any order/number)
4. Growth Check — Evolve ready Pokémon, score Rep, check end trigger
5. Dusk Cleanup — Discard hand, check Greenhouse (3+ cards = +1 token), refill Meadow, pass turn

**Costs:** Common 1 token/1 icon | Midrange 2/2 | Premium 3/3 | Hybrid 2/2 split + effect | Facility 2-4 | Spring Clean 2 tokens

**Nurture Burst:** 3+ same type on 1 Pokémon in 1 Day = +1 icon (threshold 2 with Lucario/Toy Workshop)

**End:** Final Week after 8 evolutions (2-3P) or 6 evolutions (4P) or 12 Days (solo)

**Income:** 2 + babies + evolved + facilities (cap 12) + trailing bonus

**Meadow:** 2 slots (1-3P) / 3 slots (4P). Refills on adopt + at Dusk Cleanup.
