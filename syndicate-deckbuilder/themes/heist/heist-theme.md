# Syndicate Deckbuilder System: Heist Theme

This is the thematic elaboration of the **Syndicate Deckbuilder** system, applying the core mechanics to a classic cinematic heist setting.

## 🎭 Thematic Mapping

The core, theme-agnostic mechanics of the Syndicate System map intuitively to a heist narrative.

### Core Currencies & Tension
- **Currency 1 (Physical): Gear**
  - Represents specialized equipment, weapons, tools, and hardware.
- **Currency 2 (Intangible): Intel**
  - Represents inside knowledge, forged credentials, blueprints, and passwords.
- **Tension: Heat**
  - Represents the escalating police presence, security awareness, and alarm levels. Higher Heat makes the final getaway much deadlier.

### The Four Phases
1. **Recruiting (Phase I) $\rightarrow$ The Crew:** Drafting Specialist Packs to define your starting asymmetric decks.
2. **Planning (Phase II) $\rightarrow$ The Legwork:** Acquiring new cards from the "Black Market" (Gear) or "Shady Contacts" (Intel).
3. **Execution (Phase III) $\rightarrow$ The Score:** Overcoming "Obstacle" cards (Guards, Security Systems, Vaults) to acquire "Loot" (Rewards).
4. **Extraction (Phase IV) $\rightarrow$ The Getaway:** Overcoming "Roadblock" cards using your deck. Difficulty scales precisely with accrued Heat. Dropping Loot (VP) might be required to survive.

---

## 📦 Game Components

- **Crew Boards:** Asymmetric player boards detailing the Specialist's core passive/active ability and starting Health/Capacity.
- **Market Deck (The Underworld):** The shared deck used during The Legwork.
- **Obstacle Deck:** The central challenges faced during The Score. Divided by difficulty or location tier (e.g., Lobby, Security, The Vault).
- **Loot Deck:** The Rewards. Includes Art, Cash, Data Drives, Diamonds, each with different VP values and maybe minor passive effects.
- **Heat Track:** A shared or personal dial/board tracking accumulated Tension.

## 👥 Character Archetypes & Starting Decks

Each player begins with a 10-card starting deck uniquely tailored to their role in the heist. All starter decks contain basic resource generation, plus a few unique abilities.

### **The Hacker**
*Bypasses digital security systems, manipulates data, and interacts with the mainframe.*
- **Passive Skill:** *Override* - Whenever you overcome a "Digital" Obstacle, generate 1 extra Intel.
- **Starting Deck (10 Cards):**
  - 4x *Basic Script* (Provides 1 Intel)
  - 3x *Burner Phone* (Provides 1 Gear)
  - 2x *Social Engineering* (Provides 1 Intel or reduces Heat by 1)
  - 1x *Overclock* (Provides 2 Intel, generates 1 Heat)

### **The Grifter**
*Focuses on social engineering, disguise, and distraction to bypass human elements.*
- **Passive Skill:** *Inside Man* - You may peek at the top card of the Obstacle Deck once per turn.
- **Starting Deck (10 Cards):**
  - 4x *Smooth Talk* (Provides 1 Intel)
  - 3x *Concealed Carry* (Provides 1 Gear)
  - 2x *Misdirection* (Ignore 1 Intel or Gear requirement of a "Human" Obstacle)
  - 1x *Bribe* (Provides 1 Intel, 1 Gear, generates 1 Heat)

### **The Muscle**
*Provides physical force, intimidation, and crowd control. Loud, but effective.*
- **Passive Skill:** *Breach* - You may spend 2 Gear to permanently defeat a "Physical" Obstacle without meeting its specific requirements (generates +1 Heat).
- **Starting Deck (10 Cards):**
  - 4x *Brute Force* (Provides 1 Gear)
  - 3x *Street Smarts* (Provides 1 Intel)
  - 2x *Intimidate* (Provides 2 Gear, generates 1 Heat)
  - 1x *Smash & Grab* (Provides 3 Gear, generates 2 Heat)

### **The Fixer**
*The logistical mastermind. Excels at resource management and specialized gear procurement.*
- **Passive Skill:** *Connections* - Cards cost 1 less of either currency type in the Black Market.
- **Starting Deck (10 Cards):**
  - 3x *Blueprints* (Provides 1 Intel)
  - 4x *Supplies* (Provides 1 Gear)
  - 2x *Black Market Dealer* (Draw 1 extra card next turn during The Legwork, or provides 1 Gear)
  - 1x *Safehouse* (Reduce your Heat by 2, limit once per Execution phase)

### **The Driver (Bonus Archetype)**
*Focuses on the extraction phase, reducing risks when the alarm goes off.*
- **Passive Skill:** *Idling Engine* - You may ignore 1 "Roadblock" penalty during The Getaway.
- **Starting Deck (10 Cards):**
  - 3x *City Maps* (Provides 1 Intel)
  - 4x *Wrench* (Provides 1 Gear)
  - 2x *Evasive Maneuvers* (Reduce the Heat cost of your next action by 1)
  - 1x *Nitro* (Draw 2 cards, then discard 1)

## 📜 Thematic Rules & Nuances

- **Loud vs. Quiet Actions:** Cards in the market often have a "Loud" keyword (generates Heat but provides more power) or "Quiet" keyword (costs more Gear/Intel to acquire but generates no Heat).
- **Contraband (Loot Types):** 
  - *Cash* (Low VP, easy to carry/extract) 
  - *Gold Bullion* (High VP, but adds a penalty during The Getaway)
  - *Data Drives* (Variable VP depending on other cards collected)
- **The Alarm (Escalation Trigger):** Once the collective Heat reaches a certain threshold, the Obstacle deck might escalate to a harder tier (e.g., SWAT instead of Rent-A-Cops).
