# PokéNursery: Blissful Beginnings — Game Brief

**Players**: 1-4 | **Time**: 35-70 min | **Complexity**: Medium

You are a Chansey caretaker at Blossom Grove Nursery, nurturing baby Pokémon through care and attention until they undergo their Evolution Glow. Build your care repertoire through deck building, plan your Daily Schedule through hand management, and complete each baby's unique Growth Chart through set collection.

**Theme**: Warm, pastoral Pokémon nursery where players earn Reputation by evolving baby Pokémon, expanding their Nursery Wing, and becoming the most trusted caretaker in Blossom Grove.

**Core Mechanisms**: Hand Management (5-card flat Daily Schedule), Deck Building (Blossom Bazaar market, Stockroom Depth scoring), Set Collection (4 care types: Berry, Medicine, Song, Toy), Tableau Building (3-5 Crib nursery), Draw Engine (facilities and abilities only).

**Components**: 150 pieces — 16 double-sided baby Pokémon cards (Togepi, Pichu, Cleffa, Riolu, Happiny, and 11 more), 56 Bazaar care cards (dual-use: icons + currency), 40 starting deck cards, 12 facility cards, 4 nursery wing boards, 1 Blossom Bazaar board, accessories. No tokens — cards are the currency.

**Win Condition**: Most Reputation when the end-game triggers (8 total evolutions at 2-3P, 6 at 4P, or 12 Days solo). Reputation comes from evolutions (2-5 Rep each), facilities, Nurture Mastery bonus, care set bonuses, and the new Stockroom Depth Bonus (1 Rep per 3 Bazaar cards in your deck).

**Key Features**: Dual-use cards (tuck for icons or spend as currency), Evolution Glow flip moment, Nurture Burst combos (Togetic Harmony Bloom: +1 Rep per Burst), flat hand size (advantage from card quality not quantity), Stockroom Depth Bonus end-game scoring, Spring Cleaning deck thinning, 16 unique evolved abilities, solo Season Clock mode.

**Design Scores**: Balance 6.8/10 (Conditional Pass) | Fun 7.0/10 (Solid). Strongest dimension: Narrative (8/10). Known tuning areas: Spring Cleaning cost, Blossom Greenhouse ROI, 4P downtime.
