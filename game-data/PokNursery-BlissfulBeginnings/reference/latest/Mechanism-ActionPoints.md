# Action Points

**Taxonomy:** Action Selection > action_points | Complexity: 2

## Parameters

- **Budget:** 3 Action Points (AP) per player per turn
- **Actions Available:**
  - **Feed** (1 AP): Place 1 Food token on a baby Pokemon in your nursery.
  - **Play** (1 AP): Place 1 Joy token on a baby Pokemon in your nursery.
  - **Rest** (1 AP): Place 1 Rest token on a baby Pokemon in your nursery.
  - **Gather Supplies** (1 AP): Draw 2 Supply cards from the Supply Deck.
  - **Adopt** (1 AP): Take 1 baby Pokemon from the Meadow into your nursery (max 4 cradle slots).
  - **Use Nursery Upgrade** (0 AP, once per turn): Activate an upgrade tile ability.
- **Manager Bonus:** Each player's Chansey/Blissey grants 1 free thematic action per round.

## Justification

3 AP creates an approachable decision space that avoids analysis paralysis while making every turn meaningful. The caring actions (Feed, Play, Rest) map directly to nurturing verbs, making the mechanism invisible behind the theme. Scarcity of 3 AP forces prioritization of which babies get attention, creating gentle tension without cutthroat competition.

## Synergies

- **Set Collection:** AP spent on care actions directly advance evolution recipe completion.
- **Income:** Passive token income frees AP for non-care actions like Adopt and Gather Supplies.
