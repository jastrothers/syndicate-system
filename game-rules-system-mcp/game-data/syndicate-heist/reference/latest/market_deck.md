# Market Deck (The Underworld)

The Market Deck contains cards players draft during Phase II: The Legwork. Costs are paid in Gear, Intel, or a combination. The market row is always 5 cards.

## Example Market Cards List

### Gear-Focused
1. **Flashbang**
   - **Cost:** 3 Gear
   - **Type:** Tactic (Loud)
   - **Effect:** Reduce the Gear or Intel requirement of the next Obstacle by 2. Generates 1 Heat.
2. **Heavy Armor**
   - **Cost:** 4 Gear
   - **Type:** Equipment
   - **Effect:** Prevents the next "Wound" or Penalty card you would receive this phase from an Obstacle.
3. **Thermal Lance**
   - **Cost:** 5 Gear
   - **Type:** Tool (Loud)
   - **Effect:** Provides 3 Gear towards "Vault" or "Physical" Obstacles. Generates 2 Heat.
4. **Stun Gun**
   - **Cost:** 2 Gear
   - **Type:** Weapon (Quiet)
   - **Effect:** Provides 2 Gear towards "Human" Obstacles.
5. **Grappling Hook**
   - **Cost:** 3 Gear, 1 Intel
   - **Type:** Equipment
   - **Effect:** Ignore 1 Gear requirement of any "Security" Obstacle.

### Intel-Focused
6. **Insider Codes**
   - **Cost:** 3 Intel
   - **Type:** Data
   - **Effect:** Provides 2 Intel towards "Digital" Obstacles.
7. **Forged Keycard**
   - **Cost:** 2 Intel, 1 Gear
   - **Type:** Tool (Quiet)
   - **Effect:** Provides 2 Intel, generates no Heat.
8. **Network Backdoor**
   - **Cost:** 4 Intel
   - **Type:** Software
   - **Effect:** Draw 2 cards.
9. **Blackmail File**
   - **Cost:** 3 Intel
   - **Type:** Data
   - **Effect:** Decrease the Crew's total Heat by 1.
10. **Security Feed Loop**
    - **Cost:** 5 Intel
    - **Type:** Software (Quiet)
    - **Effect:** Delay the effect of an Obstacle's failure penalty until the end of the round.

### Hybrid / Utility
11. **Inside Man (Asset)**
    - **Cost:** 4 Any Currency
    - **Type:** Personnel
    - **Effect:** Provides 1 Gear and 1 Intel.
12. **Bribe the Guards**
    - **Cost:** 2 Gear, 2 Intel
    - **Type:** Tactic (Quiet)
    - **Effect:** Completely bypass a Tier 1 (Lobby) Obstacle without paying its requirements or taking its rewards.
13. **Extraction Vehicle**
    - **Cost:** 4 Gear, 2 Intel
    - **Type:** Vehicle
    - **Effect:** Provides +2 to the Crew's Extraction Success margin during Phase IV.
14. **Silent Alarm Jammer**
    - **Cost:** 3 Gear, 4 Intel
    - **Type:** Tool
    - **Effect:** The next time Team Heat would increase, it does not.
15. **Burn Evidence**
    - **Cost:** 2 Any Currency
    - **Type:** Tactic
    - **Effect:** Trash 1 card from your hand or discard pile (excellent for thinning Starter cards).
