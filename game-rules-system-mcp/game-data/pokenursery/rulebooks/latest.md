## Game Overview

PokéNursery: Blissful Beginnings is a cozy engine-building and nurturing game where players take on the roles of Chansey and Blissey caretakers. Your goal is to provide a loving environment for baby Pokémon, meeting their needs to help them grow and eventually evolve into their strong, happy forms.

## Thematic Brief

In the heart of the Johto region, the **Blissful Beginnings Nursery** is a sanctuary where baby Pokémon are nurtured into their strongest selves. Players take on the roles of legendary caregivers—**Chansey** and **Blissey**—managing the daily rhythms of feeding, playing, and resting to help their charges achieve their **Evolutionary Leap**. Success is measured in **Blissful Bonds**, earned when happy Pokémon graduate from your care.

## components

### Component Manifest

The game includes:
- 4 Nursery Boards: Dual-layered boards for tracking growth.
- 12 Caretaker Tokens: 8 starting Chanseys, 4 unlockable Happinys.
- 60 Baby Pokémon Cards: The heart of the nursery.
- 40 Amenity Tiles: Upgrades for your nursery layout.
- 110 Resource Tokens: Love Berries, Nurture Marks, Joy Points, and Energy.
- 8 Specialist Cards: Unique caretaker roles.
- 1 Nurture Die: For special event resolution.

## gameplay

### Turn Structure

Each round consists of three phases:
1. **Sunrise**: Refresh markets and collect income from Amenity tiles.
2. **Nurture**: Take turns placing Caretakers (Worker Placement) on stations to gather Berries, Play, or Care for Pokémon.
3. **Sunset**: Pokémon with enough Nurture Marks evolve. Evolved Pokémon earn Bliss Points and are moved to the score pile. Check for 'Crushy' status on neglected Pokémon.

## Endgame & Scoring

The game ends after 8 rounds. Players sum Bliss Points from evolved Pokémon and specialty bonuses. The player with the most Blissful Bonds wins.
