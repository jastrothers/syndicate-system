## Core Mechanics

Five interlocking mechanisms drive the nursery experience:\n\n**Hand Management** — Draw 5+ cards each turn (base 5, plus bonuses from evolved Pokémon and facilities). Each card is dual-use: tuck it under a Pokémon for its care icons, or spend it as currency (Care Value) to purchase new cards and facilities. No carry-over between turns.\n\n**Deck Building** — Start with 10 basic cards. Purchase specialized care cards (Berry, Medicine, Song, Toy) by spending cards from hand as currency. Standard shuffle-when-empty cycling. Cards spent as currency go to your discard pile and cycle back.\n\n**Set Collection** — Each baby Pokémon requires a specific combination of 3-5 care icons to evolve. Care cards played are tucked under the Pokémon, preserving progress across turns. Playing 3+ of the same type triggers a Nurture Burst bonus.\n\n**Tableau Building** — The nursery board starts with 3 slots (expandable to 5). Each slot holds one baby Pokémon accumulating care. Evolved Pokémon remain as scoring anchors with ongoing abilities and draw bonuses. Up to 3 facility upgrades available.\n\n**Draw Engine** — Base draw of 5 cards/turn, +1 per evolved Pokémon in Cribs, plus facility and ability bonuses. Evolving Pokémon directly increases your hand size, creating an engine-building arc where early investments compound.\n\n**Core Loop**: Morning Provisions (calculate draw bonuses) → Supply Run (draw cards) → Caring Hands (tuck or spend cards) → Growth Check (evolve + adopt) → Dusk Cleanup.

## Overview

**PokéNursery: Blissful Beginnings**
Welcome to Blossom Grove Nursery, where Chansey caretakers tend to baby Pokémon, nurturing them with berries, medicine, songs, and toys until they are ready to evolve. Build your care repertoire, expand your nursery, and earn the most Reputation to become the Grand Blossom Caretaker.

- **Players**: 1-4
- **Playtime**: 35-70 minutes (scales with player count)
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Core Mechanisms**: Daily Schedule (Hand Management), Blossom Bazaar (Deck Building), Care Combos (Set Collection), Nursery Wing (Tableau Building), Draw Engine (Evolve to Draw More)

## Setup

**For All Player Counts:**

1. Place the **Blossom Bazaar board** in the center of the table.
2. Shuffle all 56 **Bazaar Care Cards** (14 Berry + 14 Medicine + 14 Song + 14 Toy) into a single face-down deck. Place on the Care Card Draw Pile space.
3. Shuffle all 16 **Baby Pokémon Cards** into a face-down deck (baby side up). Place on the Baby Pokémon Draw Pile space.
4. Reveal Baby Pokémon from the draw pile to fill the **Meadow** slots: 2 slots at 1-3 players, 3 slots at 4 players.
5. Deal Care Cards face-up to fill the **Market Row**: 5 slots at 1-3 players, 6 slots at 4 players.
6. Shuffle all 12 **Facility Cards** into a face-down deck beside the board. Reveal the top 2 face-up — these are available for purchase.
7. Each player takes:
   a. 1 **Nursery Wing** board. It has 5 Crib slots: 3 unlocked (available immediately), 2 locked (unlocked by Crib Expansion facilities).
   b. 1 **Starting Deck** of 10 cards: 3 Gentle Touch, 2 Berry Snack, 2 Soothing Lullaby, 2 Rattle Toy, 1 Potion Drops. Shuffle and place face-down on the Deck Zone.
   c. 1 **Reputation Tracker** meeple on the 0 space of the Reputation Track.
   d. 1 **Quick Reference Card**.
8. Draw **5 cards** from your personal draw pile. This is your first **Daily Schedule**.

**Solo Setup (additional steps):**
9. Shuffle the 12 **Season Clock Cards** and place in a face-down stack. The Round Tracker tracks the current Day (1-12).
10. Do not use the Start Caretaker Egg.

**Multiplayer First Player:**
Most recently cared for a pet or child receives the Start Caretaker Egg. Fallback: youngest player. The Round Tracker is not used in multiplayer.

**Player Count Adjustments:**

## Turn Structure

A game is played over **Weeks** (rounds). Each Week, every player takes one **Day** (turn) clockwise from the Start Caretaker Egg holder. Each Day has 5 phases in strict order:

**Phase 1: Morning Provisions (Draw Bonuses)**
Calculate your draw total: 5 (base) + 1 per Evolved Pokémon in Cribs + facility/ability draw bonuses (Melody Room: +1, Pikachu: +1).
**Trailing Caretaker Bonus**: If you have 2+ fewer evolutions than the leader, draw +1 card per evolution deficit (max +3 bonus cards).

**Phase 2: Supply Run (Draw)**
If personal draw pile is empty, shuffle discard pile into a new draw pile. Draw cards equal to your Morning Provisions total. If draw pile and discard are both empty, play with however many cards you have.

**Phase 3: Caring Hands (Main Actions)**
Perform any number of actions in any order. Each card in hand can be used once — either tucked for icons or spent as currency:
- **Tuck a Care Card**: Place a card from hand under a Baby Pokémon. Icons count toward evolution. Gentle Touch = 1 wild icon (declare type when tucked).
- **Purchase a Care Card**: Spend cards from hand whose Care Values total the printed cost (1-3). Spent cards go to personal discard. Take purchased card from Market Row to personal discard. Refill Market Row from draw pile.
- **Adopt a Baby Pokémon**: Take from Meadow to an empty unlocked Crib (free). Refill Meadow from Baby Pokémon Draw Pile.
- **Purchase a Facility**: Spend cards from hand whose Care Values total the printed cost (2-4). Spent cards go to personal discard. Place facility in empty Facility slot (max 3). Refill display.
- **Spring Cleaning**: Spend 1 card from hand to permanently remove 1 other card from your hand (out of the game, not to discard). The spent card goes to your discard; the removed card is out of the game. Thins your deck.
- **Pass**: Stop taking actions voluntarily.

**Phase 4: Growth Check (Evolution)**
Check each Baby Pokémon: if tucked icons meet or exceed ALL printed requirements, it evolves. Flip card to evolved side (Evolution Glow). Discard all tucked cards to personal discard. Gain Reputation equal to printed value. Evolved ability activates next Day. Check end-game trigger after all evolutions resolve.

**Phase 5: Dusk Cleanup**
Discard all remaining hand cards. Check Blossom Greenhouse (if you tucked 3+ cards this Day, your next purchase next Day costs 1 less). Refill Meadow to full if any slots are empty. Pass turn clockwise.

**End of Week**: After all players complete a Day, pass Start Caretaker Egg clockwise. New Week begins.

## Action Catalog

All actions occur during the Caring Hands phase unless otherwise noted.

| Action | Cost | Effect | Restrictions | Timing |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Tuck a Care Card | 1 card from hand | Tuck under Baby Pokémon; icons count toward evolution | Must target Baby Pokémon (not Evolved). Card must be in Daily Schedule. | Caring Hands |
| Purchase a Care Card | Spend cards totaling printed Care Value cost (1-3) | Take from Market Row to personal discard; spent cards to discard; refill row | Sufficient Care Value in hand required. Cannot buy from draw pile. | Caring Hands |
| Adopt a Baby Pokémon | Free | Take from Meadow to empty Crib; refill Meadow | Must have empty unlocked Crib. Meadow must have a baby available. | Caring Hands |
| Purchase a Facility | Spend cards totaling printed cost (2-4) | Place in empty Facility slot; spent cards to discard; active immediately | Must have empty Facility slot (max 3). | Caring Hands |
| Spring Cleaning | Spend 1 card | Remove 1 other card from hand permanently (out of game); spent card goes to discard | Must have 2+ cards in hand. | Caring Hands |
| Declare Gentle Touch Type | 0 (part of Tuck) | Choose care type for wild icon | Declare at time of tucking; permanent. | During Tuck action |
| Pass | 0 | End Caring Hands voluntarily | Unspent hand cards are discarded at Dusk Cleanup. | Caring Hands |

**Hybrid Card Special Effects:**
- Herbal Compress: Draw 1 card when tucked.
- Sing-Along Story: May tuck under any Pokémon regardless of type match.
- Musical Mobile: +1 Reputation if it completes an evolution.
- Pecha Berry Remedy: 1 Berry + 1 Medicine icons (no extra effect).

**Spending Cards as Currency:** To purchase, choose cards from your hand whose Care Values sum to at least the printed cost. All spent cards go to your personal discard pile (they cycle back through your deck). Overpayment is allowed — there is no change.

## Scoring & Endgame

**Game End Trigger:**
- 2-3P: 8 total Evolved Pokémon across all players. Complete current Week, then play 1 final complete Week.
- 4P: 6 total Evolved Pokémon (same procedure).
- Solo: After 12 Days (Season Clock).

**Scoring Sources:**
1. Evolution Reputation (during play): Printed value per Evolved Pokémon (2-5 Rep).
2. Facility Reputation (game end): Printed value per Facility (1-2 Rep).
3. Togetic Bonus (during play): +1 Rep per evolution after Togetic evolved (maximum 3 bonus Rep total).
4. Musical Mobile Bonus (during play): +1 Rep per evolution completed by Musical Mobile.
5. Nurture Mastery Bonus (game end): 1 Rep per Pokémon that evolved using a Nurture Burst.
6. Care Card Collection Bonus (game end): 1 Rep per complete 4-type set tucked under un-evolved Pokémon.
7. No penalty for un-evolved Baby Pokémon.

**Tiebreakers (in order):**
1. More Evolved Pokémon.
2. Fewer total cards in personal deck (draw + discard + hand).
3. Most recent evolution (latest in turn order).
4. Shared victory.

**Solo Scoring Thresholds:**
| Reputation | Rating |
|---|---|
| 0-9 | Novice Caretaker |
| 10-14 | Bronze Caretaker |
| 15-19 | Silver Caretaker |
| 20-24 | Gold Caretaker |
| 25+ | Grand Blossom Caretaker |

## Special Rules & Keywords

**Nurture Burst**: Tuck 3+ cards of the same care type under 1 Baby Pokémon in 1 Day = +1 bonus icon of that type. Checked per Pokémon, per type, per Day. Gentle Touch counts for declared type. Lucario reduces threshold to 2 (all types). Toy Workshop reduces threshold to 2 (Toy only). Reductions do not stack below 2.

**Evolution Glow**: When a Baby Pokémon's care requirements are fully met during Growth Check, flip to evolved side. Remove all tucked cards to personal discard. Gain printed Reputation. Evolved ability active next Day.

**Trailing Caretaker Bonus**: During Morning Provisions, if you have 2+ fewer evolutions than the player with the most evolutions, draw +1 card per evolution deficit (max +3 bonus cards). This is in addition to normal draw.

**Spring Cleaning**: During Caring Hands, spend 1 card from hand to permanently remove 1 other card from your hand (out of game, not to discard). The spent card goes to your personal discard pile. Thins your deck for future draws. Must have 2+ cards in hand.

**Dual-Use Cards**: Every care card has both care icons and a Care Value (coin icon). During Caring Hands, choose for each card: tuck it (use icons) or spend it (use Care Value as currency). Cards spent as currency go to personal discard. Care Value equals icon count for standard cards (1/2/3); hybrid cards have Care Value 1.

**Evolved Pokémon Abilities (16):**
- Togetic: +1 Rep per subsequent evolution (maximum 3 bonus Rep total from this ability).
- Pikachu: Draw +1 card during Supply Run.
- Clefairy: 1/Day, treat 1 Song icon as any type.
- Jigglypuff: When tucking Song card, also tuck top card of draw pile face-down (counts as 1 wild icon).
- Hitmonchan: 1/Day, spend 1 card from hand to tuck a card from discard pile instead of hand.
- Jynx: 1/Day, look at top 3 of Care Card Draw Pile, put 1 on top.
- Electabuzz: 1/Day, 1 Medicine card provides +1 Medicine icon.
- Magmar: Your Pokémon requiring 4+ icons need 1 fewer (minimum 3).
- Chansey: 1/Day, one card you spend as currency has +1 Care Value.
- Roselia: 1/Day, when you tuck a Berry card, one card in hand gains +1 Care Value this Day.
- Lucario: Nurture Burst threshold = 2 for all Pokémon.
- Snorlax: 1/Day, 1 Berry icon counts as 2.
- Marill: 1/Day, swap 1 hand card with top of draw pile.
- Sudowoodo: Facilities provide +1 Rep each at game end.
- Mr. Mime: 1/Day, copy another Evolved Pokémon's ability in your Cribs. If Mr. Mime is your only evolved Pokémon, you may instead peek at the top 2 Baby Pokémon cards and rearrange them.
- Chimecho: 1/Day, gain +1 Rep when triggering Nurture Burst.

**Facility Effects (6 types):**
- Crib Expansion (cost 3): Unlock 1 additional Crib. 1 Rep.
- Berry Garden (cost 2): Your Berry cards have +1 Care Value when spent as currency. 1 Rep.
- Melody Room (cost 2): Draw +1 card during Supply Run. 1 Rep.
- Toy Workshop (cost 3): Nurture Burst threshold = 2 for Toy. 2 Rep.
- Healing Ward (cost 3): 1/Day, when you tuck a Medicine card, also add +1 Medicine icon. 1 Rep.
- Blossom Greenhouse (cost 3): If you tucked 3+ cards this Day, your next purchase this Day costs 1 less (minimum 0). 2 Rep.

**Wild Icons**: Gentle Touch = 1 icon of any type. Declare when tucked; permanent. Care Value 1 when spent as currency.

**Solo Season Clock**: Days 1-4 (First Light): no removal. Days 5-8 (Growing Pains): remove 1 Market Row card after refill. Days 9-12 (Grand Blossom): remove 2 cards after refill.

## Edge Cases & Clarifications

| # | Situation | Resolution |
|---|---|---|
| 1 | Care Card Draw Pile empty | Market Row not refilled. Empty slots remain. Players may still buy remaining face-up cards. |
| 2 | Baby Pokémon Draw Pile empty | Meadow not refilled. Adopt action unavailable when Meadow empty. |
| 3 | Personal draw + discard both empty | Draw 0 cards. Play Day with 0 hand cards. May still use free actions (Adopt). |
| 4 | All Cribs occupied | Cannot adopt. Evolved Pokémon still occupy Cribs. Must buy Crib Expansion for new slots. |
| 5 | No legal actions in Caring Hands | Pass. Day continues to Growth Check and Dusk Cleanup. |
| 6 | Simultaneous ability triggers | Player chooses resolution order. Resolve each fully before next. |
| 7 | Sing-Along Story usage | Provides Song + Toy icons. May tuck under any Pokémon. Non-matching icons still count. |
| 8 | Mr. Mime copies ability | Copies effect only, not identity. No retroactive bonuses. Choose target each Day. If only evolved Pokémon, use fallback peek ability instead. |
| 9 | Multiple purchases per Day | Yes, unlimited Purchase actions per Day. Each resolved individually. Must spend cards for each purchase separately. |
| 10 | Facility deck empty | No refill. Buy remaining face-up cards only. |
| 11 | Reputation exceeds 30 | Game continues. Track above 30 with reminder token. |
| 12 | Magmar + 3-icon Pokémon | Warmth only reduces 4+ icon requirements. 3-icon Pokémon unaffected. |
| 13 | Spring Cleaning requires 2+ cards | Must have at least 2 cards in hand: 1 to spend (goes to discard) and 1 to remove (out of game). |
| 14 | Trailing Caretaker Bonus tie | Only triggers if you are 2+ behind the leader. If tied for most evolutions, no bonus. |
| 15 | Meadow refill timing | Meadow refills immediately when a baby is adopted AND at Dusk Cleanup if any slots empty. |
| 16 | Overpaying for a purchase | Allowed. If you spend cards totaling more than the cost, there is no change. Plan purchases carefully. |
| 17 | Spending and tucking same card | A card can only be used once: either tucked for icons OR spent for currency, not both. |
| 18 | Berry Garden + Care Value stacking | Berry Garden adds +1 Care Value to Berry cards when spent as currency. A Berry Snack (base Care Value 1) becomes Care Value 2; a Sitrus Berry (base 2) becomes 3. |

## Quick Reference Card

**Day Summary:**
1. Morning Provisions — Calculate draw total: 5 base + 1/evolved + facility/ability bonuses. Trailing bonus: +1 draw/deficit if 2+ behind leader (max +3).
2. Supply Run — Draw cards equal to Morning Provisions total
3. Caring Hands — Tuck cards (icons) or spend cards (currency), buy cards/facilities, adopt babies, spring clean (any order/number)
4. Growth Check — Evolve ready Pokémon, score Rep, check end trigger
5. Dusk Cleanup — Discard hand, check Greenhouse (3+ tucked = next purchase -1 cost), refill Meadow, pass turn

**Care Values:** Starting cards = 1 | Common = 1 | Midrange = 2 | Premium = 3 | Hybrid = 1
**Purchase Costs:** Common 1 | Midrange 2 | Premium 3 | Hybrid 2 | Facility 2-4 | Spring Clean = spend 1 card

**Dual-Use:** Each card in hand → Tuck (use icons) OR Spend (use Care Value as currency). Not both.

**Nurture Burst:** 3+ same type on 1 Pokémon in 1 Day = +1 icon (threshold 2 with Lucario/Toy Workshop)

**End:** Final Week after 8 evolutions (2-3P) or 6 evolutions (4P) or 12 Days (solo)

**Draw Bonuses:** +1/evolved Pokémon, +1 Melody Room, +1 Pikachu. Trailing: +1/deficit (max +3).

**Meadow:** 2 slots (1-3P) / 3 slots (4P). Refills on adopt + at Dusk Cleanup.
