# Hand Management

Base hand: 5 cards, plus draw bonuses (+1 per evolved Pokémon, facility/ability bonuses, trailing caretaker bonus). Trailing Caretaker Bonus: if 2+ behind the leader, draw (deficit − 1) bonus cards (max +3) — always 1 fewer than the gap, so the leader retains a draw advantage. All unplayed cards discard at end of turn (no carry-over). Each card is dual-use: tuck under a Pokémon (use icons) or spend as currency (use Care Value). Core decision: balance tucking cards for evolution progress vs. spending them to acquire better cards from the Blossom Bazaar.
