# Income

**Taxonomy:** Economy and Resources > income | Complexity: 1

## Parameters

- **Base Income:** At the start of each round, every player gains 1 Food + 1 Joy + 1 Rest token.
- **Upgrade Income:** Token-generating nursery upgrades (Berry Garden, Playroom, Soft Beds) add to income at turn start.
- **Manager Income:** Chosen nursery manager provides 1 additional token of their specialty type per round.
- **Evolution Momentum:** For each evolved Pokemon in your Evolution Garden, gain 1 Supply card at round start.

## Justification

Income is the heartbeat of the nursery -- ensures that even without spending AP on Gather Supplies, players always make progress. Prevents the feel-bad state of a wasted turn. Escalating income from evolutions creates positive feedback accelerating the late game and preventing drag. At complexity 1, this mechanism is essentially invisible.

## Token Economy (per player, 9 rounds)

- Base income: 9F + 9J + 9R = 27 tokens
- Manager bonus: +9 tokens (specialty type)
- AP-generated tokens (max): 27 tokens
- Upgrade income (est.): ~6 tokens
- Total throughput: ~60-70 tokens per game

## Synergies

- **Action Points:** Passive income frees AP for Adopt and Gather actions.
- **Tableau Building:** Upgrade tiles augment income generation.
