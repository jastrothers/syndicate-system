# Component Manifest: Syndicate Heist

This manifest defines the physical components required to play the Heist theme for Syndicate Deckbuilder.

## 1. Specialist Packs (Starting Decks)
Total: 50 Cards (5 Packs of 10)
- **The Hacker Pack**: 10 Cards (Focus: Intel, Digital Security)
- **The Muscle Pack**: 10 Cards (Focus: Gear, Physical Security)
- **The Fixer Pack**: 10 Cards (Focus: Resource Flex, Heat Management)
- **The Grifter Pack**: 10 Cards (Focus: Intel, Social/Human Obstacles)
- **The Driver Pack**: 10 Cards (Focus: Gear, Extraction Bonus)

## 2. Market Deck (The Underworld)
Total: 40 Cards
- 15 Gear-Focused Equipment/Tools
- 15 Intel-Focused Software/Data
- 10 Hybrid Personnel/Tactics

## 3. Obstacle Decks (Challenges)
Total: 20 Cards (Tiered)
- 8 Tier 1 (Lobby/Security)
- 7 Tier 2 (Vault/Response)
- 5 Tier 3 (High-Security/Extraction)

## 4. Loot Deck (Rewards)
Total: 15 Cards
- 6 Cash Loot
- 5 Data Drive Loot
- 3 Gold Bullion Loot
- 1 Art Loot

## 5. Other Components
- 1 Heat Tracker (Tension Meter)
- 4 Player Reference Cards
- 20 Wound Cards (Minor/Major)
- 1 Rulebook
