# Hand Management

Base hand: 5 cards flat, plus draw bonuses from facilities/abilities only (Melody Room: +1, Pikachu: +1). Hand size does not scale with evolution count. All unplayed cards discard at end of turn (no carry-over). Each card is dual-use: tuck under a Pokémon (use icons) or spend as currency (use Care Value). Core decision: balance tucking cards for evolution progress vs. spending them to acquire better cards from the Blossom Bazaar. With a fixed hand of 5, efficiency of each card matters — higher-quality Bazaar cards (2-3 icons/value) outperform starting cards (1 icon/value) significantly.
