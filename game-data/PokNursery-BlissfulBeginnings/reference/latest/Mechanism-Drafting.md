# Drafting

**Taxonomy:** Action Selection > drafting | Complexity: 2

## Parameters

- **The Meadow:** Public display of 4 face-up baby Pokemon cards, refreshed from the Baby Deck when taken.
- **Supply Draft:** At round start, reveal 4 Supply cards. In turn order, each player takes 1 for free. Remainder stays for Gather Supplies action.
- **Nursery Upgrade Draft:** At the end of every 3rd round (rounds 3, 6, 9), reveal (player count + 1) upgrade tiles. Players draft one in reverse score order (catch-up mechanism). Upgrades are permanent tableau additions.

## Justification

Drafting injects player interaction without direct conflict, perfectly suited to the cozy tone. The Meadow creates moments of friendly competition over desirable babies. The Supply Draft gives tactical edge at round start. The Upgrade Draft every 3rd round provides strategic milestones that break the game into three acts. Reverse-score drafting is a built-in catch-up mechanic.

## Synergies

- **Tableau Building:** Meadow draft feeds cradle slots; Upgrade draft feeds upgrade slots.
- **Set Collection:** Choosing which babies to adopt is choosing which sets to pursue.
