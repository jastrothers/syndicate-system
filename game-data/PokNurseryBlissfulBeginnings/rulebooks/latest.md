## Core Mechanics

Five interlocking mechanisms drive the nursery experience:\n\n**Hand Management** — Draw 5 cards each turn. Choose which care cards to play on nursery Pokémon, which to hold, and which to discard. No carry-over between turns.\n\n**Deck Building** — Start with 10 basic cards. Purchase specialized care cards (Berry, Medicine, Song, Toy) and baby Pokémon from a shared market row of 5 cards (+1 Pokémon slot). Standard shuffle-when-empty cycling.\n\n**Set Collection** — Each baby Pokémon requires a specific combination of 3-5 care icons to evolve. Care cards played are tucked under the Pokémon, preserving progress across turns. Playing 3+ of the same type triggers a Nurture Burst bonus.\n\n**Tableau Building** — The nursery board starts with 3 slots (expandable to 5). Each slot holds one baby Pokémon accumulating care. Evolved Pokémon remain as scoring anchors with ongoing abilities. Up to 3 facility upgrades available.\n\n**Income** — Base 2 care tokens/round, +1 per baby Pokémon, +1 per evolved Pokémon (stacking), facility bonuses. Token cap of 12.\n\n**Core Loop**: Nurture Phase (income) → Supply Phase (draw + market) → Care Phase (play cards) → Growth Phase (evolve + adopt) → Cleanup Phase.

## Overview

**PokéNursery: Blissful Beginnings**
Welcome to Blossom Grove Nursery, where Chansey caretakers tend to baby Pokémon, nurturing them with berries, medicine, songs, and toys until they are ready to evolve. Build your care repertoire, expand your nursery, and earn the most Reputation to become the Grand Blossom Caretaker.

- **Players**: 1-4
- **Playtime**: 35-70 minutes (scales with player count)
- **Complexity**: Medium
- **Core Mechanisms**: Daily Schedule (Hand Management), Blossom Bazaar (Deck Building), Care Combos (Set Collection), Nursery Wing (Tableau Building), Morning Provisions (Income)

## Setup

**For All Player Counts:**

1. Place the **Blossom Bazaar board** in the center of the table.
2. Shuffle all 56 **Bazaar Care Cards** (14 Berry + 14 Medicine + 14 Song + 14 Toy) into a single face-down deck. Place on the Care Card Draw Pile space.
3. Shuffle all 16 **Baby Pokémon Cards** into a face-down deck (baby side up). Place on the Baby Pokémon Draw Pile space.
4. Reveal Baby Pokémon from the draw pile to fill the **Meadow** slots: 2 slots at solo, 3 slots at 2-4 players.
5. Deal Care Cards face-up to fill the **Market Row**: 5 slots at 1-3 players, 6 slots at 4 players.
6. Shuffle all 12 **Facility Cards** into a face-down deck beside the board. Reveal the top 2 face-up — these are available for purchase.
7. Place all 40 **Care Tokens** (30 x 1-value, 10 x 5-value) in a shared supply.
8. Each player takes:
   a. 1 **Nursery Wing** board. It has 5 Crib slots: 3 unlocked (available immediately), 2 locked (unlocked by Crib Expansion facilities).
   b. 1 **Starting Deck** of 10 cards: 3 Gentle Touch, 2 Berry Snack, 2 Soothing Lullaby, 2 Rattle Toy, 1 Potion Drops. Shuffle and place face-down on the Deck Zone.
   c. 1 **Reputation Tracker** meeple on the 0 space of the Reputation Track.
   d. 1 **Quick Reference Card**.
9. Players take starting **Care Tokens** based on seat order (position relative to the Start Caretaker Egg holder):
   - 1st player (Start Caretaker Egg): **2 tokens**
   - 2nd player: **3 tokens**
   - 3rd player: **4 tokens**
   - 4th player: **5 tokens**
   - Solo: **2 tokens**
10. Draw **5 cards** from your personal draw pile. This is your first **Daily Schedule**.

**Solo Setup (additional steps):**
11. Shuffle the 12 **Season Clock Cards** and place in a face-down stack. The Round Tracker tracks the current Day (1-12).
12. Do not use the Start Caretaker Egg.

**Multiplayer First Player:**
Most recently cared for a pet or child receives the Start Caretaker Egg. Fallback: youngest player. The Round Tracker is not used in multiplayer.

## Turn Structure

A game is played over **Weeks** (rounds). Each Week, every player takes one **Day** (turn) clockwise from the Start Caretaker Egg holder. Each Day has 5 phases in strict order:

**Phase 1: Morning Provisions (Income)**
Income = 2 (base) + 1 per Baby Pokémon in Cribs + 1 per Evolved Pokémon in Cribs + Facility bonuses.
**Catch-Up Bonus**: If you have fewer evolutions than the leader, gain +1 Care Token per evolution deficit (max +2 bonus). This bonus cannot trigger on consecutive Days — if you received it last Day, place any Care Token face-down on your board as a cooldown marker; remove it at the start of your following Day (before Phase 1). If the cooldown marker is present, you do not receive the Catch-Up Bonus this Day.
Take tokens from supply. Reserve cap: 12 tokens. Excess income is lost.

**Phase 2: Supply Run (Draw)**
If personal draw pile is empty, shuffle discard pile into a new draw pile. Draw up to 5 cards (hand limit). Pikachu ability: +1 card. Melody Room facility: +1 card. If draw pile and discard are both empty, play with however many cards you have.

**Phase 3: Caring Hands (Main Actions)**
Perform any number of actions in any order:
- **Play a Care Card**: Tuck a card from hand under a Baby Pokémon. Icons count toward evolution. Gentle Touch = 1 wild icon (declare type when tucked).
- **Purchase a Care Card**: Pay printed cost (1-3 tokens). Take from Market Row to personal discard. Refill Market Row from draw pile.
- **Adopt a Baby Pokémon**: Take from Meadow to an empty unlocked Crib (free). Refill Meadow from Baby Pokémon Draw Pile on adoption.
- **Advanced Care**: Pay 2 Care Tokens to place **up to 2** bonus icons (any care types, split freely) on one Baby Pokémon in your Cribs. Track each icon with a **1-value Care Token** placed on the Pokémon card. May only target 1 Baby Pokémon per Day. These icons count toward evolution.
- **Purchase a Facility**: Pay printed cost (2-4 tokens). Place in empty Facility slot (max 3). Refill display.
- **Spring Cleaning**: Pay 3 Care Tokens to remove 1 card from your hand permanently (out of the game). This thins your deck.
- **Pass**: Stop taking actions voluntarily.

**Phase 4: Growth Check (Evolution)**
Check each Baby Pokémon: if tucked icons plus Advanced Care bonus icons meet or exceed ALL printed requirements, it evolves. Flip card to evolved side (Evolution Glow). Discard all tucked cards to personal discard; return Advanced Care reminder tokens to supply. Gain Reputation equal to printed value. Evolved ability activates next Day. Check end-game trigger after all evolutions resolve.

**Phase 5: Dusk Cleanup**
Discard all remaining hand cards. Check Blossom Greenhouse (3+ cards played = gain 1 token; 5+ cards played = gain 2 tokens instead). Pass turn clockwise.

**End of Week**: After all players complete a Day, pass Start Caretaker Egg clockwise. Refill Meadow to full from the Baby Pokémon Draw Pile. New Week begins.

## Scoring & Endgame

**Game End Trigger:**
- 2-4P: 8 total Evolved Pokémon across all players. Complete current Week, then play 1 final complete Week.
- Solo: After 12 Days (Season Clock).

**Final-Week Adoption Window**: When the end-game trigger fires, immediately reveal the top 2 Baby Pokémon from the Draw Pile face-up beside the board. During the final Week only, any player who has **0 Evolved Pokémon** may adopt one of these two for free (no Meadow slot required; still requires an empty Crib). Players who adopted then evolved are not eligible — they already gained evolution value. Remove unclaimed cards at game end — they do not score.

**Scoring Sources:**
1. Evolution Reputation (during play): Printed value per Evolved Pokémon (2-5 Rep).
2. Facility Reputation (game end): Printed value per Facility (1-2 Rep).
3. Togetic Bonus (during play): +1 Rep per evolution after Togetic evolved (maximum 3 bonus Rep total).
4. Musical Mobile Bonus (during play): +1 Rep per evolution completed by Musical Mobile.
5. Leftover Tokens (game end): 3 unspent tokens = 1 Rep (rounded down).
6. No penalty for un-evolved Baby Pokémon.

**Tiebreakers (in order):**
1. More Evolved Pokémon.
2. Fewer total cards in personal deck (draw + discard + hand).
3. Most recent evolution (latest in turn order).
4. Shared victory.

**Solo Scoring Thresholds:**
| Reputation | Rating |
|---|---|
| 0-9 | Novice Caretaker |
| 10-14 | Bronze Caretaker |
| 15-19 | Silver Caretaker |
| 20-24 | Gold Caretaker |
| 25+ | Grand Blossom Caretaker |

## Special Rules & Keywords

**Nurture Burst**: Tuck 3+ cards of the same care type under 1 Baby Pokémon in 1 Day = +1 bonus icon of that type. Declare when your third matching card is tucked. Checked per Pokémon, per type, per Day. Gentle Touch counts for declared type. Lucario reduces threshold to 2 (all types). Toy Workshop reduces threshold to 2 (Toy only). Reductions do not stack below 2.

**Evolution Glow**: When a Baby Pokémon's care requirements are fully met during Growth Check, flip to evolved side. Remove all tucked cards to personal discard; return any Advanced Care reminder tokens to supply. Gain printed Reputation. Evolved ability active next Day.

**Catch-Up Bonus**: During Morning Provisions, if you have fewer evolutions than the player with the most evolutions, gain +1 Care Token per evolution deficit (max +2 bonus). **This bonus cannot trigger on consecutive Days** — when you receive it, place any Care Token face-down on your board as a cooldown marker; remove it at the start of your following Day (before Phase 1). If the cooldown marker is present when Morning Provisions begins, you do not receive the Catch-Up Bonus this Day.

**Advanced Care**: Pay 2 Care Tokens during Caring Hands to place **up to 2** bonus icons (any care types, split freely between types as you choose) on one Baby Pokémon in your Cribs. Track each bonus icon with a **1-value Care Token** placed on the Pokémon card. You may only use Advanced Care on 1 Baby Pokémon per Day. These icons count toward evolution requirements in Phase 4. Advanced Care icons do not count as tucked cards for Nurture Burst thresholds, Blossom Greenhouse card counts, or abilities referencing "tucked cards." Tokens are returned to supply when the Pokémon evolves.

**Spring Cleaning**: Pay 3 Care Tokens during Caring Hands to permanently remove 1 card from your hand (out of game, not to discard). Thins your deck for future draws. Cannot remove your last card in hand.

**Evolved Pokémon Abilities (16):**
- Togetic: +1 Rep per subsequent evolution (maximum 3 bonus Rep total from this ability).
- Pikachu: Draw +1 card during Supply Run.
- Clefairy: 1/Day, treat 1 Song icon as any type.
- Jigglypuff: When tucking Song card, also tuck top card of draw pile face-down (counts as 1 wild icon).
- Hitmonchan: 1/Day, pay 1 token to tuck a card from discard pile instead of hand.
- Jynx: 1/Day, look at top 3 of Care Card Draw Pile, put 1 on top.
- Electabuzz: 1/Day, 1 Medicine card provides +1 Medicine icon.
- Magmar: Your Pokémon requiring 4+ icons need 1 fewer (minimum 3).
- Chansey: +1 Care Token during Morning Provisions.
- Roselia: Once per Day (not once per Berry-card-tuck), gain 1 Care Token when you tuck a Berry card. Triggers at most once per Day regardless of how many Berry cards you tuck.
- Lucario: Nurture Burst threshold = 2 for all Pokémon.
- Snorlax: 1/Day, 1 Berry icon counts as 2.
- Marill: 1/Day, swap 1 hand card with top of draw pile.
- Sudowoodo: Facilities provide +1 Rep each at game end.
- Mr. Mime: 1/Day, copy another evolved Pokémon's ability in your Cribs. If no other evolved Pokémon, peek the top 2 Baby Pokémon and rearrange them.
- Chimecho: 1/Day, gain +1 Rep when triggering Nurture Burst.

**Facility Effects (6 types):**
- Crib Expansion (cost 3): Unlock 1 additional Crib. 1 Rep.
- Berry Garden (cost 2): +1 income during Morning Provisions. 1 Rep.
- Melody Room (cost 2): Draw +1 card during Supply Run. 1 Rep.
- Toy Workshop (cost 3): Nurture Burst threshold = 2 for Toy. 2 Rep.
- Healing Ward (cost 3): 1/Day, treat 1 Berry card you tuck as both a Berry and a Medicine icon (counts as 2 icons of 2 types). 1 Rep.
- Blossom Greenhouse (cost 3): If 3+ cards were played in Caring Hands this Day, gain 1 token at Dusk Cleanup; if 5+ cards were played, gain 2 tokens instead. All cards count toward this threshold — including Gentle Touch and hybrid cards. 2 Rep.

**Wild Icons**: Gentle Touch = 1 icon of any type. Declare when tucked; permanent.

**Care Token Cap**: Maximum reserve of 12 tokens at all times.

**Solo Season Clock**: Days 1-6 (First Light): no removal. Days 7-12 (Grand Blossom): remove 1 Market Row card after each refill.

**Solo Clarifications**: The Catch-Up Bonus and Final-Week Adoption Window do not apply in solo mode. Solo end condition is determined solely by the Season Clock (12 Days).

|---|---|---|

| 4 | All Cribs occupied | Cannot adopt from Meadow or Final-Week Window. Evolved Pokémon still occupy Cribs. Must buy Crib Expansion for new slots. |

## Quick Reference Card

**Day Summary:**
1. Morning Provisions — Collect tokens (2 + babies + evolved + facilities, cap 12). Catch-Up Bonus: +1/deficit if behind the leader (max +2); no consecutive Days (place any Care Token face-down as cooldown).
2. Supply Run — Draw to 5 cards
3. Caring Hands — Play cards, buy cards/facilities, adopt babies, advanced care (2 tokens = up to 2 icons on 1 baby/Day), spring clean (3 tokens) — any order, any number
4. Growth Check — Evolve ready Pokémon (tucked + Advanced Care icons), score Rep, check end trigger
5. Dusk Cleanup — Discard hand, check Greenhouse (3+ cards played = +1 token; 5+ = +2 tokens; all card types count), pass turn

**Week End:** Pass Caretaker Egg → Refill Meadow to full.

**Costs:** Common 1 token/1 icon | Midrange 2/2 | Premium 3/3 | Hybrid 2/2 split + effect | Facility 2-4 | Spring Clean 3 tokens | Advanced Care 2 tokens (up to 2 bonus icons; 1 baby/Day)

**Nurture Burst:** 3+ same type on 1 Pokémon in 1 Day = +1 icon (threshold 2 with Lucario/Toy Workshop). Declare when third matching card is tucked.

**End:** Final Week after 8 evolutions (all player counts) or 12 Days (solo). Final-Week Window: reveal top 2 babies; players with 0 evolved Pokémon may take 1 free.

**Income:** 2 + babies + evolved + facilities (cap 12) + catch-up bonus (max +2)

**Seat Tokens:** 1st=2 | 2nd=3 | 3rd=4 | 4th=5

**Meadow:** 2 slots (Solo) / 3 slots (2-4P). Refills on adopt + at Week end.

**Reminders:** Catch-Up Bonus cooldown = any Care Token face-down on board | Advanced Care icons = 1-value tokens on Pokémon card
