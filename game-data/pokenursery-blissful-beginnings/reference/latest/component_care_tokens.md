# Care Tokens

**40 tokens total, shared supply**

| Denomination | Material | Size | Color | Count |
|-------------|----------|------|-------|-------|
| 1x | Wooden disc | 15mm | Pink | 30 |
| 5x | Wooden disc | 15mm | Dark pink | 10 |

## Usage
- Generated during Morning Provisions (income mechanism)
- Spent to purchase Care Cards and Facility Cards from Blossom Bazaar
- Player cap: 8 tokens maximum at any time
- 1 Care Token = 1 Wild care icon (expensive conversion via Azurill/Marill evolved ability only)

## Income Formula
Morning Provisions = 2 (base) + 1 per Baby Pokemon + 1 per Evolved Pokemon + Facility bonuses. Cap 8.
