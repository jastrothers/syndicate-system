### Component Manifest

# Component Manifest

'Baddies: The Board Game' includes the following physical components:

## Player Components
- 6 Persona Boards: Large double-sided boards featuring a Baddie character and a Reputation Track.
- 6 Reputation Tokens: Small wooden tokens matching the player colors.
- 6 Persona Markers: Matching the player colors for location placement.

## Cards
- 80 Drama Cards: Standard-sized cards used for actions and scoring.
- 12 Location Cards: Determines which locations are active each episode.

## Tiles and Boards
- 8 Location Tiles: Hexagonal tiles representing show venues.
- 18 Episode Segment Tiles: Small tiles for the Airing phase.
- 1 Episode Tracker: Tracking 6 Episodes of gameplay.
- 1 Screen Time Board: Central tracker for player influence.

## Tokens and Dice
- 100 Clout Tokens: Cardboard tokens in denominations of 1, 5, and 10.
- 1 Shade Die: A custom 8-sided die for the 'Turning Up' mechanic.
- 1 First Baddie Marker: A gold microphone token.

### Game Rules and Phases

# How to Play

A game of 'Baddies: The Board Game' is played over a series of 6 Episodes on the Episode Tracker.

## Episode Structure

### 1. Booking Phase
The First Baddie flips two cards from the Location Cards deck. Players take their Persona Boards and place their Persona Marker on one of the active Location Tiles. Only 2 players can occupy the same location.

### 2. Filming Phase
In turn order, players perform actions on their Persona Boards from the following:
- **Go Live**: Play one card from your hand of Drama Cards. Resolve effect. Update the Screen Time Board as needed.
- **Turn Up**: Roll the Shade Die. 
    - *Success (1-6)*: Gain that many Clout Tokens.
    - *Bust*: Lose 2 Reputation Tokens and second action.
- **Vibe Check**: Discard Drama Cards. Gain 1 Reputation Tokens for every 2 cards.
- **Confessional**: Draw back up to hand size of 5 Drama Cards.

### 3. Editing Phase
Players select one Episode Segment Tiles using their dial.

### 4. Airing Phase
Reveal dials.
- Solo segment: gain 4 Clout Tokens and 1 Reputation Tokens.
- Shared segment: gain 2 Clout Tokens and 1 Reputation Tokens.

## Winning the Game
After 6 Episodes, gain bonus Clout Tokens for:
- Total Reputation: 1 Clout Token per Reputation Token earned.
- Highest Reputation Tokens: 10 Clout Tokens.
- Most Location Tiles visited: 5 Clout Tokens.
- Set Collection: 3 Clout Tokens per pair of matching iconic line cards.

The player with the most Clout Tokens wins using the First Baddie Marker as a trophy!
