# Obstacle Deck (The Score)

During Phase III: Execution, the crew encounters these challenges. They must pay the Requirement to get the Reward (or bypass it), or suffer the Penalty.

## Tier 1: The Lobby (Easy)

1. **Rent-A-Cop**
   - **Type:** Human
   - **Requirement:** 2 Gear OR 2 Intel
   - **Reward:** 1 Intel OR trash 1 Starter card
   - **Penalty:** Take 1 "Minor Scrape" (Wound) card.

2. **Locked Service Door**
   - **Type:** Physical
   - **Requirement:** 3 Gear
   - **Reward:** Gain 1 Cash Loot card.
   - **Penalty:** +1 Heat.

3. **Reception ID Check**
   - **Type:** Human/Digital
   - **Requirement:** 3 Intel
   - **Reward:** Look at the top 2 cards of your deck, put them back in any order.
   - **Penalty:** +1 Heat, discard 1 card.

4. **Basic Keypad**
   - **Type:** Digital
   - **Requirement:** 2 Intel, 1 Gear
   - **Reward:** Draw 1 card.
   - **Penalty:** +1 Heat.

5. **Nervous Employee**
   - **Type:** Human
   - **Requirement:** 1 Gear, 1 Intel
   - **Reward:** Gain 1 Cash Loot card.
   - **Penalty:** +2 Heat (they panicked and hit the alarm).

---

## Tier 2: Security Network (Medium)

6. **Laser Grid**
   - **Type:** Physical
   - **Requirement:** 4 Gear, 1 Intel
   - **Reward:** Gain 1 Data Drive Loot card.
   - **Penalty:** +2 Heat, take 1 "Minor Scrape".

7. **Armed Patrol**
   - **Type:** Human
   - **Requirement:** 5 Gear
   - **Reward:** Gain 1 Cash Loot card, trash 1 card.
   - **Penalty:** Take 1 "Gunshot Wound" (-1 Capacity) card, +1 Heat.

8. **Biometric Scanner**
   - **Type:** Digital/Physical
   - **Requirement:** 4 Intel, 2 Gear
   - **Reward:** Gain 1 Data Drive Loot card.
   - **Penalty:** +2 Heat.

9. **Server Firewall**
   - **Type:** Digital
   - **Requirement:** 5 Intel
   - **Reward:** Gain x2 Data Drive Loot cards.
   - **Penalty:** Discard your hand, draw +1 card next turn.

10. **Camera Blindspot**
    - **Type:** Tactic 
    - **Requirement:** 2 Intel, 2 Gear
    - **Reward:** Reduce Heat by 1.
    - **Penalty:** +1 Heat.

---

## Tier 3: The Vault (Hard)

11. **Titanium Blast Door**
    - **Type:** Physical
    - **Requirement:** 8 Gear, 2 Intel
    - **Reward:** Gain 1 Gold Bullion Loot card.
    - **Penalty:** +3 Heat, the heist is stalled (skip your next Phase II).

12. **Quantum Encryption**
    - **Type:** Digital
    - **Requirement:** 8 Intel, 2 Gear
    - **Reward:** Gain 1 Art Loot card OR x2 Data Drive Loot cards.
    - **Penalty:** +3 Heat.

13. **SWAT Response Team**
    - **Type:** Human
    - **Requirement:** 6 Gear, 4 Intel
    - **Reward:** Gain 1 Gold Bullion Loot card, reduce Heat by 1.
    - **Penalty:** Take 2 "Gunshot Wound" cards, +2 Heat.

14. **Motion Sensors (Loud)**
    - **Type:** Physical/Digital
    - **Requirement:** 5 Gear, 5 Intel
    - **Reward:** Gain 1 Art Loot card.
    - **Penalty:** If not completed, permanently adds +2 to Extraction Base Difficulty.

15. **The Inside Man Betrayal**
    - **Type:** Human
    - **Requirement:** 7 Intel
    - **Reward:** Gain 1 Art, 1 Gold Bullion Loot cards.
    - **Penalty:** Lose all Intel currently generated this turn, +2 Heat.
