# Mechanism: Push Your Luck
ID: push_your_luck
Description: Players can 'Turn Up the Heat' during filming to multiply their 'Clout' gain, but if they roll too high on the 'Shade Die', they 'Bust' and lose a turn.
Justification: Captures the risk-taking nature of reality TV fame-seeking.
Complexity: 2
